I am a Senor Associate Professor at Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE),India.
My area of clinical expertise is Adult Neurogenic Language (communication) Disorders, specifically Aphasia.
My research interests include normal and disordered language processing in the brain, orthographic processing in the brain, cognitive-linguistics, bilingualism, and cognitive ageing.
